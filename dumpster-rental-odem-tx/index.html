Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does it cost to rent a dumpster in Houston?

Dumpster rental cost varies based on size, rental period, and type of debris. We provide upfront, transparent quotes for services in Houston and all our service areas with no hidden fees.

2. What size dumpster do I need for a home cleanout in Odem, TX?

For a full home cleanout in Odem, TX, a 20-yard dumpster is often ideal. For garage or single-room cleanouts, a 10-yard or 15-yard dumpster is usually sufficient.

3. What can I not put in a rental dumpster?

Hazardous materials like paints, chemicals, batteries, tires, and appliances are prohibited. Heavy materials like pure dirt or concrete may have special pricing or weight limits.

4. How long can I keep the dumpster?

Standard rental periods are typically 7-10 days. We offer flexible rental periods and can often arrange extensions if needed.

5. How does the delivery and pickup process work?

We schedule a delivery time, our driver will place the dumpster in your specified location. Once you’ve filled it, you call to schedule pickup, and we will haul it away.
